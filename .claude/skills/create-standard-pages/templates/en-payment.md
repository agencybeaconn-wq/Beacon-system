## 2. Currency

The checkout displays prices in your detected local currency. The actual charge appears on your statement in the store's original currency — conversion is handled by your card issuer using their exchange rate. No hidden markups from us.

## 3. Payment approval timeline

| Method | Approval time |
|---|---|
| Credit / debit card | Instant to 2 hours |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay / Shop Pay | Instant |
| PayPal | Instant |
| PIX (Brazil) | Instant |
| Bank transfer | 1-3 business days |
| Klarna / Afterpay | Instant (subject to provider approval) |

For **unapproved payments** (insufficient funds, wrong data, fraud analysis), we notify you by email with instructions to retry.

## 4. Transaction security

4.1. **No card data is stored on our servers**. All payments are processed through **PCI DSS Level 1 certified** gateways with end-to-end encryption.

4.2. Transactions undergo **automatic fraud analysis**. High-risk orders may be blocked or require additional verification (ID document). For erroneous blocks, contact **{{support_email}}** for manual review.

4.3. **We never ask for your password, CVV, or banking credentials via email, WhatsApp, or phone.**

See [Secure Checkout](/pages/secure-checkout) for details.

## 6. Cancellation before shipping

To cancel an order before dispatch:

1. Email **{{support_email}}** with order number and reason (urgent!)
2. If not yet shipped, we cancel and process a refund
3. If already shipped, please await delivery and follow return procedure

**Response time:** within 1 business day.

## 7. Cancellation by the store

{{client_name}} reserves the right to cancel orders in cases of:

- Fraud detection or reasonable suspicion
- Manifest pricing error (e.g., typo listing a product for a fraction of its value)
- Stock rupture identified after checkout
- Shipping to regions not served by available carriers

In all cases, a full refund is processed per the timelines in Section 9.

## 8. Right of withdrawal

Consumers have the right to withdraw from the purchase within:

- **14 days** in EU/UK (per EU Consumer Rights Directive)
- **30 days** in US (our policy)
- **7 days** in Brazil (per CDC Art. 49)

See procedure in [Shipping](/pages/shipping#returns).

## 10. Chargebacks and disputes

If you notice an unrecognized charge:

1. **Contact us first** at **{{support_email}}** — most cases are resolved faster directly than through the bank
2. If you prefer to file a chargeback via your bank/card network, note that the process can take **30-90 days**
3. {{client_name}} cooperates fully with legitimate investigations, providing all necessary documentation

**Fraudulent chargebacks** (disputing legitimate purchases that were delivered) may result in blacklisting and notification to credit bureaus.
