## 🛍️ Products

### Are the products authentic?

Yes — 100%. Every item sold by **{{client_name}}** is genuine, sourced directly from authorized suppliers and licensed manufacturers. We never sell replicas or counterfeits.

### How do I choose the right size?

Each product page has a detailed size chart with measurements in centimeters and inches (chest, length, sleeve). Measurements are taken with the garment flat, not stretched.

Pro tip: lay out a jersey that fits you well, measure it, and compare with the chart. Still unsure? WhatsApp us at **{{support_phone}}** — we help choose.

### Do products have warranty?

Yes — **90-day manufacturing warranty** from delivery date. If you spot a defect:

1. Email **{{support_email}}** with photos/video and order number
2. We respond within 3 business days
3. Options: replacement of same item, swap for equal value, or full refund

### The product I want is sold out. What now?

Many products restock within 7-30 days. On sold-out product pages, click **"Notify me when available"** and we add you to a priority list.

### Do you offer customization (name and number)?

Depends on the collection. Products with customization show the field on the product page. Production time adds **2-4 business days**. Note: customized products are **not eligible** for the right of withdrawal (14-day return) but manufacturing warranty applies normally.

### How many installments can I split into?

- **EU / UK:** Klarna (3-6 installments) or Afterpay

### Is the site secure for purchases?

Yes. We use **TLS 1.3 encryption**, **PCI DSS Level 1 certified** payment gateways, and comply with **GDPR, LGPD, and CCPA**. Card data is never stored on our servers. More details in [Secure Checkout](/pages/secure-checkout).

### What currency am I charged in?

The checkout shows prices in your detected local currency. The actual charge from your card/bank appears in the original store currency — your bank handles the conversion using their rate. No hidden markups from us.

### Do you offer free shipping?

Yes — on orders above certain thresholds (varies by country). The free shipping cupom auto-applies at checkout when the minimum is met.

### How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you receive:

- **Email** with tracking number and carrier link
- **SMS/WhatsApp** (if authorized) with status updates
- Always available on your account at **{{shop_url}}/account**

First tracking update can take up to 48h after shipment — this is normal.

### What about customs and import duties?

For international orders, customs duties and import taxes may apply upon delivery and are the **buyer's responsibility**. We declare the actual product value on the customs form — we cannot mark items as "gift" or undervalue them.

Check your country's customs policy before ordering. Many countries have low-value thresholds below which no duties apply.

### My package hasn't arrived. What do I do?

1. Check the tracking for the latest update
2. If stuck for more than 7 business days, email **{{support_email}}**
3. We open an inquiry with the carrier
4. If lost, we ship a replacement or refund in full within 10 business days of confirmation

## 🔁 Returns & refunds

### What's your return policy?

- **EU / UK:** 14 days from delivery (per consumer protection law)
- **US:** 30 days from delivery (our policy)
- **Brazil:** 7 days from delivery (per CDC Art. 49)
- **Rest of world:** 14 days

Product must be unused, with original tags and packaging. Return shipping is the buyer's responsibility unless the product was defective.

### How do I request a return?

Email **{{support_email}}** with:

- Order number
- Product(s) to return
- Reason (size, color, defect, change of mind)
- Photos (if defect)

We respond within 2 business days with return instructions. Don't ship the product until you hear back.

### How long do refunds take?

| Payment method | Refund timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit card | 5-14 business days (varies by bank) |
| PayPal | 3-7 business days |
| Bank transfer | 5-10 business days |
| Buy Now Pay Later | Per provider's policy |

### Can I exchange for a different product?

Yes:

- **Higher value:** you pay the difference
- **Lower value:** we issue a credit (valid 90 days) or refund the difference

### Can customized products be returned?

Only for **manufacturing defects** (90-day warranty applies). They're excluded from the right of withdrawal since they're made-to-order.
