## 7. Customs and import duties

For **international orders**, customs duties and import taxes may apply upon delivery and are the **buyer's responsibility**. Key points:

- We declare the **actual product value** on customs forms — we cannot mark items as "gift" or undervalue
- Each country has different rules and thresholds (some allow low-value exemptions)
- Payment of duties is required before the carrier releases the package
- {{client_name}} is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance
- We recommend checking your country's import rules before ordering

## 8. Incorrect address or failed delivery

8.1. It is the customer's responsibility to provide a correct, complete, and accessible delivery address. Errors may cause:

- Package return to sender (new shipment charged separately)
- Significant delays
- In extreme cases, loss of package (not covered by {{client_name}} if due to address error)

8.2. In case of **recipient absence**:

- Most carriers attempt 2-3 redelivery attempts
- After failed attempts, package goes to local depot/post office for pickup
- If not collected in time, package returns to our warehouse

## 9. Delays due to external factors

{{client_name}} is not liable for delays caused by:

- Carrier strikes or operational issues
- Severe weather events
- Customs processing times
- Road blockages, public manifestations, government actions
- Pandemics and public health emergencies

We maintain proactive communication and seek alternatives whenever possible.

## 10. Damaged or lost packages

### 10.1 Package arrived damaged (visible damage to box or product):

- **Refuse the delivery** whenever possible
- If accepted, photograph the packaging and product within **48 hours** of receipt
- Email evidence to **{{support_email}}** with order number
- We initiate replacement or full refund procedure

### 10.2 Lost package (no tracking updates for 7+ business days):

- Email **{{support_email}}** with order number
- We open an investigation with the carrier
- If confirmed lost, we ship a replacement or issue full refund within **10 business days** of confirmation

## 11. Returns

### 11.1 Right of withdrawal

- **EU / UK:** 14 days from delivery (per EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013) — no reason needed
- **US:** 30 days from delivery (our policy, not statutory)
- **Brazil:** 7 days from delivery (per CDC Art. 49)
- **Rest of world:** 14 days from delivery (our policy)

### 11.2 Conditions

- Product must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags and accessories
- Customized items (with custom names, numbers, or patches) are excluded per consumer law
- Return shipping is the buyer's responsibility **unless** the product was defective

### 11.3 How to initiate

Email **{{support_email}}** with:

- Order number
- Item(s) to return
- Reason
- Photos (especially for defects)

We respond within **2 business days** with return instructions.
